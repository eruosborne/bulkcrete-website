About BULKCRETE

Gold Coast Concreters Who Get It Done

BULKCRETE was built on a simple idea — do the job properly, every time. Based in Coomera on the Gold Coast, we've spent over 5 years pouring concrete for homeowners and developers right across the region.

We're not a faceless company with a call centre. We're a tight crew of experienced concreters who show up on time, communicate clearly, and leave every site clean. Whether it's a driveway in Hope Island or a full slab in Pimpama — we treat every job the same way: with respect for the customer's property and a commitment to quality finish.

From plain grey slabs to exposed aggregate and pool surrounds — if it involves concrete on the Gold Coast, we can handle it.
